Features

Everything your mouse can do

One activation key. Full mouse control. No hardware required.

🎯
Smooth Cursor Movement

Velocity-based movement with natural acceleration. The cursor picks up speed as you hold the key and stops smoothly when you release.

🖱️
Click & Drag

Press the click key to click. Hold it while moving to drag — resize windows, select text, rearrange files, all without a mouse.

📜
Smooth Scrolling

Scroll any page or document with dedicated scroll keys. Speed scales with how long you hold, just like a real scroll wheel.

⚡
Turbo Mode

Hold Shift alongside any movement or scroll key to jump to 3× speed. Great for crossing a large display quickly.

⚙️
Fully Configurable

Every key binding is customizable. Change the activation key to any key on your keyboard. Adjust speed and scroll rates with sliders.

🔒
Private by Design

No internet connection. No telemetry. No analytics. Everything runs locally. The source code is open for anyone to inspect.

How it works

Three steps, every time

1
Hold the activation key

Hold your configured key (default: Right ⌘). The menu bar icon changes to 🖱 to confirm mouse mode is active.

→
2
Move, click, or scroll

Use WASD or arrow keys to move the cursor. Press Space to click. Hold Space while moving to drag. Use E/Q to scroll.

→
3
Release to return

Let go of the activation key and you're back to normal typing instantly. Zero friction, zero delay.

Installation

Up and running
in seconds

1

Download & unzip

Download MouseKeyboard.zip from the latest release and unzip it.

2

Move to Applications

Drag MouseKeyboard.app to your Applications folder.

3

Grant Accessibility permission

Open the app and follow the prompt to grant Accessibility in System Settings → Privacy & Security. Quit and reopen once done.

4

You're done

The ⌨ icon appears in your menu bar. Hold Right ⌘ and start moving.
